Tour Củ Chi Tunnels 1N

In the morning, the tour guide and driver will pick you up at the hotel (in District 1) around 7:30 AM–8:00 AM to go to Cu Chi Tunnels.

The tour guide will share a brief information about Cu Chi Tunnels first. We will take 1.5–2 hours to get to the tunnels.

When we arrive, in front of us there are a massive aircraft, tanks and a helicopter—perfect spots for photos—before entering the tunnels.

Next, we head into the tunnel system. On the way you’ll see the air-vent shafts that once supplied oxygen. The secret entrance is a narrow, “rabbit-hole” path that’s the highlight experience.

Inside you’ll discover multiple underground bunkers: fighting bunker, kitchen bunker, shelter bunker, meeting bunker, hospital bunker, and more.

Itinerary

7:30–8:00 AM: Pick-up & Departure

Guide and driver collect you at your hotel lobby and depart for Cu Chi.

9:30–10:00 AM: Outdoor Exhibits

Photo stops with vintage aircraft, tanks and helicopter on display.

10:00 AM–12:00 PM: Tunnel Exploration

Enter the secret entrance, see ventilation shafts and visit all bunker types.

12:00 PM: Return to Ho Chi Minh City

Transfer back to your hotel. Tour ends.

Inclusions / Exclusions

Included: Round-trip transport, entrance fees, English-speaking guide, water.

Not included: Lunch, personal expenses.

Tour Bảo Lộc 3N2Đ

Bảo Lộc City is located in Lâm Đồng Province in the Central Highlands, about 220 km from Ho Chi Minh City. This is a 3-day, 2-night tour.

Day 1

7:00 AM–10:30 AM: Depart HCM → Nam Cát Tiên (1 rest stop).
10:30 AM–12:30 PM: Bike 10 km inside the park to see a 500-year-old tung tree, cool stream, wildlife.
12:30 PM–1:30 PM: Lunch at local restaurant.
1:30 PM–3:00 PM: Drive to Bảo Lộc, check in hotel & rest.
7:00 PM–9:00 PM: Night food tour by motorbike.

Day 2

8:00 AM–10:00 AM: Explore Đambri Waterfall (60 m) + Alpine Coaster (1 650 m incl.).
12:00 PM–1:00 PM: Lunch.
1:00 PM–3:00 PM: Visit Tu Viện Bát Nhã pagoda.
3:00 PM–5:00 PM: Tour historic tea factory (built 1924).
7:00 PM: Dinner on your own.

Day 3

5:30 AM–7:00 AM: Sunrise “cloud-hunting” on Đại Bình Mountain (1 200 m) + coffee/tea.
7:00 AM–8:00 AM: Breakfast & check-out.
8:00 AM–10:00 AM: Visit ethnic weaving village.
10:00 AM onward: Return HCM (2 rest stops, lunch not included).

Inclusions / Exclusions

Included: Modern transport, English/Vietnamese guide, park & coaster tickets, 2 lunches, 1 breakfast, coffee/tea.
Not included: Dinner Day 2, Lunch Day 3, personal expenses.

Tour Đồng bằng sông Cửu Long 1N

Start from Ho Chi Minh City in the morning, go directly to the Mekong Delta—the richest culture in Vietnam. This one-day tour is packed with authentic experiences.

It takes 1.5–2 hours to reach the Delta. First, board a local boat to cruise the inland canals (Dragon, Unicorn, Turtle, Phoenix).

  • Đờn ca tài tử: UNESCO intangible heritage since 2013. Live traditional music in a coconut-leaf stilt-house.
  • Beekeeping: Sample honey tea from local longan-flower honey.
  • Coconut Candy Factory: Watch hand-made candy in action; taste fresh puffs in pure, chocolate, ginger, taro, peanut & coffee flavors.
  • Fruit Garden: Stroll among longan, plum, jackfruit, durian, coconut, cacao & rambutan trees.
  • Sampan Boat Ride: Glide through the coconut-water jungle.

Sample Schedule

8:00 AM–9:30 AM: Transfer

Bus via Mỹ Thuận Bridge, short rest stop.

9:30 AM–11:00 AM: Fruit Garden & Honey Tasting

Visit orchard, try longan-flower honey tea.

11:00 AM–12:00 PM: Traditional Music & Stilt-House

Enjoy Đờn ca tài tử performance.

12:00 PM–1:00 PM: Lunch at Local Restaurant
1:00 PM–2:00 PM: Coconut Candy Demonstration

Handmade candy tasting.

2:00 PM–3:00 PM: Sampan Ride

Scenic boat trip through water coconut groves.

3:00 PM–5:00 PM: Return to Ho Chi Minh City

Inclusions / Exclusions

Included: Transport, boat ride, English-speaking guide, entrance fees, honey tea, coconut candy, lunch.
Not included: Personal expenses, optional drinks.
